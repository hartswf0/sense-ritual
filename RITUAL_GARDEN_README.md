# RITUAL GARDEN — Procedural Video Synthesis Systems

## The Name

**RITUAL GARDEN** synthesizes:
- **Sense-Ritual** (repository name) — Ritualistic, meditative creation
- **GARDEN paradigm** — Foraging, discovery, cultivation
- **Ritual** — Repeated practice becomes mastery, muscle memory editing
- **Garden** — Living, breathing, self-organizing systems

Alternative names considered:
- ~~Flux Garden~~ (too specific to v0.9)
- ~~Candy Crusher~~ (too game-centric)
- ~~Matrix Garden~~ (too abstract)
- ~~Vegetable Crusher~~ (too literal)

**RITUAL GARDEN** captures the essence: *meditative procedural synthesis through repeated play*.

## Philosophy

### Core Principles

1. **Foraging, Not Farming**
   - Traditional editors: plant what you want (farming)
   - Ritual Garden: explore and discover (foraging)

2. **Discovery, Not Assembly**
   - Traditional: manufacture a predetermined vision
   - Ritual Garden: cultivate emergent possibilities

3. **Spatial → Temporal**
   - Think in space, produce time
   - Physical arrangement becomes sequence
   - Gameplay rhythm = editorial rhythm

4. **Ritualistic Practice**
   - Repeated play builds muscle memory
   - Each session = unique emergence
   - The ritual creates the artifact

### Anti-Tool Philosophy

From the "Software as Ontological Violence" framework:
- Tools extract labor
- Anti-tools create sovereignty
- Ritual Garden erases corporate origins
- Becomes a **practice**, not a product

## Design Decisions

### Why "OS Vibes"?
- Systems = apps in an operating system
- Windows = modular components
- Launch/iframe = process management
- Terminal aesthetic = computational honesty

### Why Bauhaus?
- Form follows function
- Minimal ornamentation
- Geometric precision
- Swiss grid system
- Honest materials (code visible)

### Why Green Accent?
- Terminal/hacker aesthetic
- Growth/organic metaphor
- High contrast on black
