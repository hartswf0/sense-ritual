Objective

Defend against hostile narrative fragments from "Can't Get You Out of My Head" documentary. Deploy defensive systems to protect your noosphere from ideological collapse.

Timeline System

When fragments hit your walls, they form horizontal timelines. Each clip's width represents its video duration. The golden playhead scans across active timelines. Fragments collectively damage walls.

AOE (175): Area disruption
