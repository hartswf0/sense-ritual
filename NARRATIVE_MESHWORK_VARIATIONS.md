# 5 NARRATIVE MESHWORK VARIATIONS

## Concept
Combine meshwork-725's breathing video mosaic with narrative garden's 3-act structure in 5 distinct ways.

---

## 1. **narrative-meshwork-01.html** — Beat-Driven Breathing
**Concept:** Each collected beat changes the breathing rhythm and intensity

**Features:**
- Act 1 (Setup): Slow, calm breathing (0.0008 speed)
- Act 2A (Confrontation): Faster, urgent breathing (0.0015 speed)
- Act 2B (Crisis): Erratic, tense breathing (variable speed)
- Act 3 (Resolution): Deep, satisfied breathing (0.0005 speed)
- Visual bloom increases with dramatic tension

**Mechanic:** Match videos → collect narrative beats → breathing adapts to story

---

## 2. **narrative-meshwork-02.html** — Syntagma Visual Modes
**Concept:** Each syntagma type applies different visual treatment

**Syntagma Effects:**
- **AS (Autonomous Shot)**: High contrast, isolated cells
- **PS (Parallel Syntagma)**: Split-screen style, dual displays
- **BS (Bracket Syntagma)**: Rapid cuts, quick flashes
- **DS (Descriptive Syntagma)**: Soft focus, dreamy bloom
- **ALT (Alternating)**: Checkerboard pattern reveal
- **SC (Scene)**: Continuous flow, no borders
- **ES (Episodic)**: Grid divisions, chapter markers
- **OS (Ordinary)**: Linear progression, left-to-right

**Mechanic:** Choose syntagma → visual style changes → affects gameplay feel

---

## 3. **narrative-meshwork-03.html** — Act-Based Color Palettes
**Concept:** Color scheme shifts as you progress through acts

**Act Palettes:**
- **Act 1 (Setup)**: Cool blues/cyans (introductory, establishing)
- **Act 2A (Confrontation)**: Warm yellows/oranges (rising tension)
- **Act 2B (Crisis)**: Hot reds/magentas (peak conflict)
- **Act 3 (Resolution)**: Balanced purples/greens (harmony restored)

**Mechanic:** Collecting beats from each act unlocks that palette
- Visual representation of narrative progression
- Symbols change color to match act
- Bloom intensity reflects dramatic structure

---

## 4. **narrative-meshwork-04.html** — Temporal Beat System
**Concept:** Videos play segments that match narrative time position

**Time Mapping:**
- Beat 1 (Opening Image) → All videos start at 0:00
- Beat 5 (Debate) → Videos at 25% mark
- Beat 9 (Midpoint) → Videos at 50% mark (MIDPOINT!)
- Beat 12 (Dark Night) → Videos at 75% mark
- Beat 15 (Final Image) → Videos at 100% (ending)

**Mechanic:** As you collect beats, ALL videos jump to that temporal position
- Creates synchronized narrative progression across mosaic
- Videos "age" with the story
- Visual coherence through time

---

## 5. **narrative-meshwork-05.html** — Storyboard Assembly
**Concept:** Build visual storyboard while playing, see act structure emerge

**Layout:**
- Main game: 70% of screen (breathing mosaic)
- Side panel: 30% storyboard view
- Each collected beat = thumbnail in storyboard
- Thumbnails arranged in 3-act visual flow

**Features:**
- Drag to reorder beats (remix narrative)
- Hover to preview video segment
- Export as storyboard PDF
- Visual "story spine" emerges
- Color-coded by act
- Shows suggested syntagmas with icons

**Mechanic:** Match-3 gameplay BUILDS the storyboard in real-time
