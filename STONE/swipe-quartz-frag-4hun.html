How It Works

Welcome to Fragment Flow!

This app is designed to help you construct a narrative by curating and engaging with film fragments.

1. Swipe Fragments: Swipe right to collect a fragment, swipe left to skip it. Each fragment represents a core concept or moment from a film.

2. Compile Souls: In the 'Compiler Lab', you can compile a 'soul' for your collected fragments. A soul is a rich character profile generated by an AI compiler, based on the fragment's details and a chosen interpretive framework (e.g., Stanislavski, Plot Dynamics).

3. Chat with Fragments: In the 'Chat' section, you can interact directly with your collected fragments. Each fragment (especially those with a compiled soul) will respond as a distinct persona, challenging your narrative choices. You have 5 turns to convince them to 'JOIN' your sequence.

4. Build Your Sequence: Your collected fragments form your 'Collection'. Arrange them to build a cohesive narrative. The goal is to craft a compelling sequence and achieve high 'Coherence'!

5. Export & Share: Export your progress and compiled sequences for further use or analysis.

Have fun flowing with your fragments!

About Fragment Flow

Fragment Flow is an experimental tool exploring narrative construction, AI-driven character generation, and interactive storytelling.

It simulates the process of an editor assembling a film by having users interact with individual filmic "fragments" as if they were sentient actors or narrative elements.

Core Concept: Inspired by the idea of method acting and the intentionality behind every cinematic choice, each fragment can be given a 'soul' via various AI 'compilers'. These souls then drive the fragment's personality and responses during chat interactions.

Technology: Built with pure HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. The AI compilation and chat responses are simulated locally by default, but can be configured to use a real OpenAI API key for live AI generation (use with caution regarding API key exposure).
