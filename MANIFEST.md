## 🎯 RECOMMENDED WORKFLOW

### For Creating Narrative Films:
1. **Start:** `narrative-meshwork-organic.html`
2. **Play:** Match-3 cells to collect narrative beats
3. **Watch:** Click "WATCH FILM" to see assembled sequence
4. **Automate:** Click "AUTOMATE" for cellular automata mode
5. **Export:** (Future) Save assembled film data

### For Experimenting with Visuals:
1. **Start:** `meshwork-725.html`
2. **Adjust:** Bloom, edges, blend modes
3. **Observe:** Breathing patterns
4. **Settings:** Real-time visual control

### For Understanding Structure:
1. **Start:** `narrative-garden-v03.html`
2. **Learn:** 15-beat structure
3. **Practice:** Syntagma selection
4. **Analyze:** Act progression
